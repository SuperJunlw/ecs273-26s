Title: Abbott Earnings Beat Estimates—but It Was All Downhill From There
Ticker: JPM
Published: 2026-04-16T21:15:00
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/m/c42b4049-f158-3a12-8ffa-4b61a80701d8/abbott-earnings-beat.html?.tsrc=rss

Abbott Laboratories posted first-quarter earnings that edged past analysts’ estimates but a protracted slump in one business line and a dilutive acquisition sent shares tumbling on Thursday. Abbott reported adjusted earnings of $1.15 a share, beating Wall Street’s calls for $1.14. Sales rose 3.7% on a comparable basis to $11.16 billion, narrowly ahead of the $11 billion analysts had projected.
