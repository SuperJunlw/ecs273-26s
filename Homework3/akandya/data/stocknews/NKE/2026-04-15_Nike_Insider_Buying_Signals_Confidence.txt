Title: Nike Insider Buying Signals Confidence
Ticker: NKE
Published: 2026-04-15T17:20:17
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/nike-insider-buying-signals-confidence-162017445.html?.tsrc=rss

This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
Nike (NKE) rose 2.81% in pre-market trading after filings showed Nike CEO Elliott Hill and Apple (NASDAQ:AAPL) CEO Tim Cook both purchased Nike shares.
Cook bought 25,000 shares at an average price of $42.43 on April 10, 2026, lifting his holdings to 130,480 shares, worth roughly $5.5 million. Hill purchased 23,660 shares on April 13, 2026 at $42.27, increasing his stake to 265,247 shares, worth roughly $12 million valued at current prices. Both transactions were reported via Form 4 filings with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.
Both transactions were executed around the $42 level, near its 52-week lows, in a moment that the stock is down over 30% year to date. The buys come as Nike continues to navigate softer demand trends, particularly in its key China market, alongside an ongoing turnaround effort under Hill's leadership.
