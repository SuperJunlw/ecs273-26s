Title: Apple CEO Tim Cook Buys Nike Stock. His Last Purchase Didn’t Go So Well.
Ticker: NKE
Published: 2026-04-15T18:45:00
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/m/dccf6e49-6d51-3d12-af5e-a9833089111b/apple-ceo-tim-cook-buys-nike.html?.tsrc=rss

While Wall Street has been sprinting away from Nike stock this year, recent purchases by Apple CEO Tim Cook and the apparel maker’s chief executive signal some insiders are betting on a comeback. Cook purchased 25,000 Nike shares on Friday for roughly $42.43 each, according to a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, for a total of $1.06 million. The Apple CEO serves as Nike’s lead independent director and has sat on the board for more than a decade.
