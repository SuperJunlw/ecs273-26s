Title: Tech Stocks Power S&P 500 and Nasdaq to Records
Ticker: CAT
Published: 2026-04-15T21:44:00

Surging tech stocks carried the S&P 500 and Nasdaq composite to new records on Wednesday, a milestone in major indexes’ rebound from war-fueled losses. Buoyed by big gains from the likes of Microsoft and Tesla the S&P 500 rose 0.8% to 7022.95—rallying into the close to finish comfortably above its previous all-time high set in January. More recently, though, investors have grown increasingly confident that the U.S. and Iran will eventually reach a peace deal.
