Title: Stellantis Expands Microsoft AI Deal, Stock Remains Flat
Ticker: MSFT
Published: 2026-04-16T18:01:38
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/stellantis-expands-microsoft-ai-deal-170138314.html?.tsrc=rss

This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
Stellantis (STLA, Financials) is expanding its long-term cooperation with Microsoft to implement over 100 artificial intelligence initiatives across its business to boost performance.The deal focuses on improving consumers, operations, and engineering. Both companies bring automotive expertise and cloud-based technologies. Microsoft's AI, cybersecurity, and Azure cloud services will be integrated by Stellantis. To help digital cars, it wants a global cyberdefense center.Market reaction has been muted. Shares mainly stayed the same after the announcement, suggesting investors want more proof that the plan will improve finances.Stellantis struggled in 2025, so people are cautious. After cutting electric vehicle plans by 22 billion, it lost money. The company changed its strategy after miscalculating how quickly customers would migrate to electric automobiles.As part of the revamp, Stellantis is shifting its infrastructure to the cloud. It wants to minimize data center operations by 60% by 2029 and give employees AI tools like Microsoft 365 Copilot. Cooperation promotes efficiency and digital capacity, but investors may wait until rewards appear.
