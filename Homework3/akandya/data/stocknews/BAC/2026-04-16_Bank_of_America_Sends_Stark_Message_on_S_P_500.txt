Title: Bank of America Sends Stark Message on S&P 500
Ticker: BAC
Published: 2026-04-16T18:00:43
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/bank-america-sends-stark-message-170043744.html?.tsrc=rss

This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
Bank of America Securities said the S&P 500 appears elevated on most valuation measures, though it warned that historical comparisons may not fully capture today's market dynamics, according to a Thursday client note.
The firm said the S&P 500 trades above typical levels on 17 out of 20 metrics it monitors. In several cases, valuations also exceed levels seen during the dot-com era, reflecting strong investor positioning and sustained equity demand.

Key indicators remain well above long-term norms. The cyclically adjusted price-to-earnings ratio, or Shiller P/E, stands near 38.5 versus an average of 17.6. Price-to-book is around 5.15 compared with 2.78 historically, while the market-cap-to-GDP ratio is roughly 1.78, more than double its long-run level.
However, Bank of America noted the S&P 500 has evolved structurally, with higher-margin sectors and different business models, which may limit direct comparisons with earlier cycles.
