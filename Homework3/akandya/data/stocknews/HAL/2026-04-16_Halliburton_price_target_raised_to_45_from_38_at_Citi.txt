Title: Halliburton price target raised to $45 from $38 at Citi
Ticker: HAL
Published: 2026-04-16T13:00:11
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/news/halliburton-price-target-raised-45-120011390.html?.tsrc=rss

Citi analyst Scott Gruber raised the firm’s price target on Halliburton (HAL) to $45 from $38 and keeps a Buy rating on the shares. The firm adjusted targets in the oil and gas equipment and services group as part of a Q1 preview. The sector has “cross-currents” with the completion market recovering and companies with Middle East exposure brining uncertainty, the analyst tells investors in a research note.
