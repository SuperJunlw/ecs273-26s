Title: Latest News In Energy Transition - Statkraft Advances Wind Farm Repowering in Norway
Ticker: CVX
Published: 2026-04-16T12:37:34
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/sectors/energy/articles/latest-news-energy-transition-statkraft-113734385.html?.tsrc=rss

Statkraft has submitted its first licence application for the repowering of an existing wind farm in Norway, marking a significant step in the nation's energy transition efforts. The application, concerning the Hitra wind farm, involves the installation of fewer but more efficient turbines to nearly double the current electricity production while minimizing environmental impact. This repowering initiative aligns with the need for increased renewable energy production to support the energy transition, as wind power is viewed as a crucial technology that can quickly boost electricity generation at a sustainable cost. The project, well-received by the local municipality, is expected to meet the electricity needs of approximately 16,000 Norwegian households and provide economic benefits to the community.
