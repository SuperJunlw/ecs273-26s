Title: AMD Stock Closes at All-Time High in Longest Winning Streak Since 2005
Ticker: META
Published: 2026-04-16T21:39:00
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/m/f033ff75-39da-3ddc-9bf3-5b7931c00e2c/amd-stock-closes-at-all-time.html?.tsrc=rss

While Nvidia recent winning streak was grabbing headlines, Advanced Micro Devices has been on a tear of its own—closing at another all-time high Thursday. AMD stock closed up 7.8% at $278.26 Thursday afternoon. Thursday’s 12th consecutive rise marks its longest winning streak since 2005.
