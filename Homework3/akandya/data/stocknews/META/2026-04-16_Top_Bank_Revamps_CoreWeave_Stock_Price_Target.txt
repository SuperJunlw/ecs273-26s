Title: Top Bank Revamps CoreWeave Stock Price Target
Ticker: META
Published: 2026-04-16T18:42:11
URL: https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/top-bank-revamps-coreweave-stock-174211974.html?.tsrc=rss

This article first appeared on GuruFocus.
CoreWeave (NASDAQ:CRWV) received a raised price target from Bernstein SocGen Group following two new customer agreements, though the firm kept its cautious rating on the stock.
The investment firm lifted its target on CoreWeave to $67 from $56 while maintaining an Underperform rating. CoreWeave shares have climbed about 29% over the past week and recently traded near $110.

Bernstein cited a $21 billion incremental contract signed with Meta (NASDAQ:META) and a separate deal with Anthropic for an undisclosed sum. The analyst noted that CoreWeave has exceeded early 2026 expectations with these new customer additions.
Meta and CoreWeave expanded their existing relationship with the multibillion-dollar agreement. The Anthropic deal marks a new customer logo for CoreWeave, though Bernstein characterized the size as likely modest.
The firm acknowledged that near-term performance for CoreWeave and other neocloud providers may remain strong. However, Bernstein reiterated its Underperform stance on CoreWeave, suggesting longer-term concerns could surface later despite the recent deal momentum.
